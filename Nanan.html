Data analytics project development enhancing public transportation efficiency and accessibility through data analysis.

Public transportation is getting a data-powered makeover! Data science analyzes massive amounts of information (smartcard data, sensor data, etc.) to:

  • Predict demand: Know where and when people need transport.
  • Optimize routes and schedules: Reduce wait times and improve efficiency.
  • Predict maintenance needs: Avoid disruptions and keep things running smoothly.
